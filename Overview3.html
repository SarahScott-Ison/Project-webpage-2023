Some Information on the Bands


Green Day are an American rock band. Their lead singer is Joe Armstrong. The band formed in 1987.

Korn are an American Nu Metal band, orginally started performing in 1993. Their lead singer is Jonathan Davis.

Red Hot Chili Peppers are an American Rock band, formed in 1983. The singer is Anthony Kiedis.
